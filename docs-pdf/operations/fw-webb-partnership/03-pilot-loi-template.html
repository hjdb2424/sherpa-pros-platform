Letter of Intent — Sherpa Pros LLC × F.W. Webb Company

Pilot Hub Co-Location Program (12-Month Pilot)

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") is entered into as of {{effective_date}} by and between:

  • Sherpa Pros LLC, a {{state_of_formation}} limited-liability company with principal office at {{sherpa_address}} ("Sherpa Pros"), and
  • F.W. Webb Company, a Massachusetts corporation with principal office at 200 Merrimac Street, Newburyport, MA 01950 ("FW Webb")

(each a "Party" and collectively the "Parties").

1. Pilot Scope

1.1 Pilot Program

The Parties intend to establish a 12-month pilot (the "Pilot") in which Sherpa Pros operates Sherpa Hub locations co-located inside five (5) FW Webb branches in the New England region.

1.2 Pilot Branches

The five (5) pilot branch locations (each a "Pilot Branch") shall be selected by mutual written agreement of the Parties, with the working initial slate being:

  1. FW Webb — Plaistow, NH
  2. FW Webb — Haverhill, MA
  3. FW Webb — Manchester, NH
  4. FW Webb — Woburn, MA
  5. FW Webb — Warwick, RI

Substitutions permitted by mutual written consent if a branch's facilities cannot accommodate the Sherpa Hub footprint described in §2.1.

1.3 Pilot Term

The Pilot shall commence on the earlier of (i) the soft-open date of Pilot Hub #1 or (ii) the date six (6) months after LOI execution (the "Pilot Start Date"), and shall continue for twelve (12) months ("Pilot Term"), subject to renewal or expansion as set forth in §10.

2. Sherpa Hub Footprint Within Each Pilot Branch

2.1 Footprint [LEGAL — sub-lease vs license-to-occupy classification]

Each Sherpa Hub shall occupy approximately eight hundred (800) rentable square feet within or directly adjacent to the corresponding Pilot Branch. The exact location, layout, and entry/exit configuration for each Pilot Branch shall be documented in a Branch Site Schedule to be finalized within sixty (60) days of LOI execution.

2.2 Hub Functions

Each Sherpa Hub shall include, at minimum:

  • Pro lounge / coffee / printing / phone area
  • Sherpa Materials staging area for orchestrated material orders (per §5)
  • Sherpa Pros member check-in counter
  • Hub Manager workstation
  • Member meeting / training space (sized per branch)

2.3 Buildout

Sherpa Pros shall be solely responsible for Hub buildout costs (estimated $80K per Hub, see 04-fw-webb-economic-model.md). Sherpa Pros shall use FW Webb's preferred contractor list where commercially reasonable.

3. Economics [LEGAL]

3.1 Sub-Lease Fee

Sherpa Pros shall pay FW Webb a sub-lease fee of one thousand five hundred dollars ($1,500) per Hub per month during the Pilot Term, payable monthly in advance, beginning on each Hub's individual Hub Open Date.

3.2 Common-Area Maintenance / Utilities

The sub-lease fee in §3.1 is inclusive of Hub-allocable share of building common-area maintenance, utilities (electric, water, HVAC, sewer), parking, exterior lighting, security monitoring, snow removal, and landscaping. Sherpa Pros shall be responsible for janitorial within the Hub footprint and any Hub-specific telecommunications + internet beyond the branch baseline.

3.3 Sherpa Materials Preferred-Vendor Commitment

Sherpa Pros agrees that during the Pilot Term, in the geographic regions surrounding each Pilot Branch:

  • FW Webb shall be designated the primary supply partner for the Sherpa Materials engine for SKUs that FW Webb stocks
  • Sherpa Pros shall route orders to FW Webb for any qualifying SKU where FW Webb's landed cost is within seven percent (7%) of the next-best supplier
  • Target Year-1 Sherpa Materials orchestration volume per Hub: a minimum of $20,000/month by pilot Month 6, ramping to $40,000/month by pilot Month 12 (see 09-pilot-success-metrics.md for full gates)

4. Services Provided by FW Webb

FW Webb shall provide, at no additional cost beyond §3.1, in respect of each Pilot Branch:

  • Floor space matching the Branch Site Schedule (§2.1)
  • Standard branch security monitoring + alarm system extension to Hub footprint
  • Restroom + breakroom access for Sherpa Pros members + Hub staff
  • Loading-dock access during branch hours for Sherpa Materials inbound + outbound
  • Branch parking access for Sherpa Pros members + Hub staff
  • Reasonable cooperation in joint marketing within the Pilot Branch (signage, on-counter displays — subject to mutual content review)

5. Services Provided by Sherpa Pros

Sherpa Pros shall be solely responsible for:

  • Hiring, employing, and managing the Hub Manager + all Hub staff (Sherpa Pros employees, not FW Webb employees)
  • Hub equipment, fixtures, furniture
  • Hub-staged materials inventory (Sherpa Materials buffer stock + pickup-ready items per Hub spec)
  • Sherpa Pros member acquisition, onboarding, and ongoing engagement
  • All Hub-side marketing materials + member communications
  • Maintaining all Sherpa Pros operational software (Marketplace, Materials engine, Hub dashboard)
  • Carrying commercial general liability insurance with FW Webb named as additional insured ($2M per occurrence / $4M aggregate minimum) — [LEGAL: confirm exact coverage requirements]

6. Sherpa Materials Integration [LEGAL — data sharing]

6.1 Order Routing

Sherpa Materials engine shall route eligible material orders to the corresponding FW Webb Pilot Branch for fulfillment under the preferred-vendor terms in §3.3.

6.2 Order Modes

Three fulfillment modes per Hub (per Hub spec §13):

  1. Buffer-stocked (40% of volume) — Sherpa Pros maintains buffer of high-velocity SKUs in Hub
  2. Counter-pickup (35% of volume) — Pro picks up at FW Webb counter, Sherpa Materials pre-stages
  3. Staged-and-Uber-Direct (25% of volume) — Sherpa Materials stages at Hub, Uber Direct delivers to job site

6.3 Volume Reporting

Each month FW Webb shall provide Sherpa Pros with anonymized Pilot Branch-level summary data on Sherpa Materials-attributed sales volume. Each month Sherpa Pros shall provide FW Webb with Hub member count + materials throughput metrics for the corresponding Branch Site.

7. Exclusivity (During Pilot Term) [LEGAL]

7.1 FW Webb Exclusivity

During the Pilot Term, FW Webb shall not host, sublease to, or enter into any analogous co-location partnership with any competing pro-marketplace platform (defined as any service that dispatches paid trade jobs to independent trade pros) in any of the Pilot Branches or within twenty-five (25) miles of any Pilot Branch.

7.2 Sherpa Pros Exclusivity

During the Pilot Term, Sherpa Pros shall not co-locate any Sherpa Hub with any competing trade-supply chain (defined as a multi-branch wholesale plumbing/HVAC/PVF distributor with $100M+ annual revenue) within twenty-five (25) miles of any Pilot Branch.

7.3 Carve-outs

Single-trade specialty distributors (e.g. fire-protection-only, controls-only) and DIY retail (e.g. Home Depot, Lowe's) are not "competing" under this §7. [LEGAL: refine carve-out list]

8. Performance Gates

The Parties shall jointly review Pilot performance against the gates set forth in 09-pilot-success-metrics.md, summarized:

  • Per-Hub: 40+ active members by Month 6, 80+ by Month 12; member NPS >50; materials volume $20K+/mo by M6, $40K+/mo by M12; on-time delivery >95%
  • Network-wide: 5 Hubs operational by Month 6 of Pilot; cumulative members 200+ by Month 12; cumulative materials volume $1.5M+ by Month 12
  • FW Webb side: Pilot Branch foot-traffic increase >15%; supply purchase increase >25% in pilot regions; branch NPS improvement >10 pts

Quarterly business reviews ("QBRs") shall be held by joint executive sponsors of both Parties.

9. Expansion Option

9.1 Post-Pilot Expansion

If the Pilot meets or exceeds the gates in §8, the Parties intend (subject to definitive agreements) to expand the program to twenty-five (25) co-located Sherpa Hubs within FW Webb branches by the end of Pilot-Year-2, with potential to scale to fifty (50)+ Hubs nationally by Pilot-Year-4.

9.2 Right of First Refusal

For each potential expansion Hub branch identified by Sherpa Pros, FW Webb shall have a thirty (30)-day right of first refusal to host the Hub on terms substantially similar to the Pilot. If FW Webb declines or fails to respond within thirty days, Sherpa Pros may pursue a standalone or alternate-host Hub at that branch's market.

9.3 If Pilot Underperforms

If the Pilot meets less than 70% of the §8 gates, the Parties shall meet within sixty (60) days of the Pilot Term end to determine whether to (i) extend the Pilot for an additional six months with revised gates, (ii) wind down the Pilot per §11, or (iii) renegotiate the program structure.

10. Term, Termination, Renewal [LEGAL]

10.1 LOI Term

This LOI shall remain in effect from the Effective Date until the earlier of: (i) execution of a definitive Master Services + Sub-Lease Agreement between the Parties superseding this LOI; (ii) the conclusion of the Pilot Term; or (iii) termination per §10.2.

10.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this LOI on ninety (90) days' written notice if the other Party materially breaches and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice of the breach.

10.3 Termination for Convenience

After Pilot Month 9, either Party may terminate this LOI on one hundred eighty (180) days' written notice for any reason.

10.4 Hub Wind-Down on Termination

On termination, Sherpa Pros shall remove all Hub equipment, signage, and inventory and restore the Pilot Branch footprint to substantially its pre-buildout condition within ninety (90) days, normal wear-and-tear excepted.

11. Intellectual Property [LEGAL]

11.1 Pre-Existing IP

Each Party retains all right, title, and interest in its pre-existing intellectual property. No license is granted hereunder except as expressly set forth.

11.2 Sherpa Pros Software + Brand

The Sherpa Pros marks (Sherpa Pros, Sherpa Hub, Sherpa Materials), the Sherpa Pros marketplace platform, dispatch software, Materials engine, and all Hub operational software are and shall remain the sole property of Sherpa Pros.

11.3 FW Webb Marks + Branch IP

The FW Webb marks, branch trade dress, and customer-list data are and shall remain the sole property of FW Webb. Joint marketing materials shall require prior written approval of both Parties.

11.4 Member Data

Sherpa Pros member data is owned solely by Sherpa Pros. FW Webb pro-account-holder data is owned solely by FW Webb. Aggregate, anonymized cross-data sharing per §6.3 is permitted; identifiable cross-data sharing requires the affected pro's prior written consent.

12. Confidentiality

The Parties shall execute a separate Mutual NDA covering all Pilot-related confidential information shared. Information disclosed during LOI negotiation prior to the Mutual NDA shall be treated as confidential under the same terms. [LEGAL — attach NDA as Exhibit A]

13. Non-Binding Nature of LOI [LEGAL]

Except for §7 (Exclusivity), §11 (IP), §12 (Confidentiality), §13 (this section), and §14 (Governing Law), this LOI is non-binding and reflects the Parties' good-faith intent to negotiate a definitive Master Services + Sub-Lease Agreement consistent with these terms. The aforementioned binding sections shall survive termination of this LOI.

14. Governing Law + Dispute Resolution [LEGAL]

This LOI shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles. Disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Boston, MA under JAMS Comprehensive Arbitration Rules.
