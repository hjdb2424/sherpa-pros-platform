## What this job actually is

You've held a torch and a P&L. We're looking for the rare operator who's done both — someone who came up through a licensed trade (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, carpentry, or mechanical), then stepped off the tools into branch management, ops, or service-company leadership.

We're standing up Hub #1 of a national licensed-trade marketplace. The Hub is a 3,000–4,000 sq ft physical proof point at our HQ campus in Atkinson — part dispatch nerve center, part vendor counter, part trade-pro lounge, part live demo of what Sherpa Pros looks like when it works. You will own the room.

This is not a corporate branch-manager role. There is no regional VP above you. There is Phyrom (founder) and there is you. If you need permission to repaint the wall, the answer is "send me the Sherwin-Williams code." If a master electrician walks in pissed off about a dispatch, you handle it the way you'd handle it at any shop you've run — directly, on the spot, no script.

---

## What you'll own (Day 1 → M4 grand-open)

- **Stand up the Hub.** Architect's plans land in your inbox in M0–M1. From M2 forward, you're the one walking the contractor through punch lists, signing for equipment deliveries, and making sure the POS, signage, dispatch desk, and pro lounge are ready for soft-open.
- **Hire the floor.** 2–3 hourly counter/dispatch staff before grand-open. Your call on who.
- **Build the local pro network.** We need 50+ licensed pros (NH/MA/ME) signed up and active before grand-open. You know these people, or you know who knows them. That matters.
- **Run the room post-open.** Daily ops, vendor relationships, dispatch escalations, P&L ownership for the Hub.

---

## Day-1 outcome we're hiring for

By Phase 1 M4 (grand-open week), Hub #1 is operational: pros are dispatching jobs through the platform, the counter is moving inventory, the lounge has regulars, and the room runs without you needing Phyrom in it. That's the bar.

## Required

- **5+ years in a licensed trade** — electrical, plumbing, HVAC, carpentry, mechanical, or general construction. We want someone who's actually swung tools, pulled permits, signed off on inspections, dealt with AHJs.
- **2+ years in operations, branch management, service-company leadership, or supply-house counter management** after leaving the field. P&L responsibility preferred.
- **Comfortable in a startup.** No HR department, no IT helpdesk, no procurement team. You'll order your own laptop and figure out the dispatch software.
- **Located within 45 minutes of Atkinson, NH** or willing to relocate. This is not remote.

## Strongly preferred

- **Active NH-region pro network** — you know master plumbers in Nashua, electricians in Salem, HVAC techs in Lawrence. Bonus if you've been the person they call when they need a referral.
- **Held a state trade license** (NH, MA, or ME), even if currently inactive.
- **Direct experience with one of:** F.W. Webb, Granite City Electric, Standard Electric, Wolseley, HD Supply, Ferguson, Home Depot Pro, or comparable supply-house operations.
- **Bilingual English/Spanish** — significant portion of the regional trade workforce, and we want the Hub to feel right to all of them.

## Nice to have

- Trade-school instructor experience (you can run a counter clinic).
- Have run a service-truck fleet of 3+ vehicles.
- POS / dispatch software comfort (Service Titan, Housecall Pro, BuildOps, etc.).
