## Verification protocol

This pipeline was assembled from public sources (firm websites, Crunchbase, PitchBook, BuiltWorlds, TechCrunch, press releases) current as of 2026-04. Many firm rosters change quarterly. Every partner row is treated as one of:

- **CONFIRMED** — partner name appears on firm website team page or in a 2024–2026 dated press release.
- **[VERIFY: ...]** — partner name was in research output but not cross-confirmed against the firm's own team page; before Phyrom emails, recheck the firm's `/team` page.

**Before any outreach, Phyrom should:** (a) re-pull the team page for the target firm, (b) confirm partner is still active, (c) confirm spelling, title, and LinkedIn handle.

## Status legend

- **NEW** — researched, not yet contacted
- **WARMING** — warm-intro path identified, intro request not yet sent
- **INTRO SENT** — warm-intro request sent to mutual contact, awaiting forward
- **MEETING SCHEDULED** — partner has accepted call
- **MET** — first call complete
- **DD** — in due diligence
- **TS** — term sheet conversation
- **PASS** — declined
- **HOLD** — no fit / parked

## What is NOT in this pipeline (intentionally)

- **General consumer VCs** (a16z consumer, Greylock consumer) — Sherpa Pros is a vertical marketplace, not consumer. Wrong audience for Phase 0.
- **Pure proptech VCs targeting REIT/CRE** (Fifth Wall, MetaProp directly) — MetaProp via Phase 3 NYC accelerator (per spec §6.5), not direct seed. Fifth Wall is too late-stage for Phase 0.
- **EU/Asia-focused funds** (other than Foundamental's NA partner) — distance and time-zone overhead doesn't pay off at pre-seed.
- **Generalist tier-1 funds** (Sequoia, a16z, Benchmark, Accel, Lightspeed) — their seed checks come post-traction. Park for Series A consideration in Phase 2 (M10–11).
