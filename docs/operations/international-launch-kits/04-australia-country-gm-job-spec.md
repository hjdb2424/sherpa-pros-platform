# Australia Country General Manager — Sherpa Pros Australia

> Instantiated from `01-country-gm-job-spec-template.md`. Australia (AU) is **third international country**, locked as Year 3. **Master-franchisee model is the leading hypothesis** for Australia given geography + market size.

## Title

**Australia Country General Manager (GM) — Sherpa Pros Australia**

## Reports to

- Sherpa Pros Chief Operating Officer (COO).
- Quarterly read-out to CEO Phyrom + Board.

## Location

- **Sydney, New South Wales (NSW), on-site at Sherpa Pros Australia office** (Surry Hills / Pyrmont preferred).
- Travel ~30-50% across Melbourne (Victoria (VIC)), Brisbane (Queensland (QLD)), Perth (Western Australia (WA)), Adelaide (South Australia (SA)); semi-annual to United States headquarters (HQ).

## Employment type

- Full-time salaried employee of **Sherpa Pros Australia Pty Ltd** (a proprietary limited company under the Corporations Act 2001).
- Equity participation via Sherpa Pros HQ stock plan.

## Compensation envelope (Australia-specific)

| Component | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Cash base | **AUD $230,000 – $380,000** | USD-equivalent ≈ $150-250K |
| Annual performance bonus | 30% – 50% of base | Tied to Australia GMV, pro count, NPS |
| Equity grant | 0.25% – 0.50% of fully diluted Sherpa Pros HQ shares | 4-year vest, 1-year cliff |
| Benefits | 11.5% Superannuation Guarantee (SG) employer contribution (statutory minimum, 2026 rate), private health insurance allowance, 4 weeks annual leave | Australian standard |
| Relocation | Up to AUD $90,000 | If relocating to Sydney metro |

## Required experience

- **10+ years Australian construction industry leadership**, OR **8+ years construction + 4+ years marketplace / Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) leadership**.
- Demonstrated **Profit & Loss (P&L) ownership** of AUD $30M+ annual revenue line of business.
- **Local trade industry network** — relationships with Bunnings, Reece, Tradelink, Mitre 10, Total Tools, hipages, ServiceSeeking, Master Builders Australia, Housing Industry Association (HIA).
- Day-1 ability to navigate **Australian regulatory regime**: Privacy Act 1988, Australian Consumer Law, Franchising Code of Conduct (mandatory).
- **Master-franchise governance experience strongly preferred** (since Australia is the leading candidate for master-franchisee partnership).
- **English fluency required.**
- Bachelor's degree minimum; Australian Master of Business Administration (MBA) (Australian Graduate School of Management (AGSM), Melbourne Business School (MBS), Macquarie) a plus.

## Australia regulatory regime — required day-1 knowledge

| Domain | Statute / regulator | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Data privacy | **Privacy Act 1988** + Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) | Pro and client personal data |
| Consumer protection | **Australian Consumer Law (ACL)** under Competition and Consumer Act 2010, Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC) | Online marketplace consumer protection |
| Franchise regulation | **Franchising Code of Conduct (mandatory under ACCC)** — disclosure document, cooling-off period, marketing fund rules | Master-franchisee structure heavily regulated; specialist franchise counsel mandatory |
| Worker classification | Australian Taxation Office (ATO) tests for contractor vs employee, Fair Work Act 2009, Independent Contractors Act 2006 | Pro classification |
| Tax | Goods and Services Tax (GST) 10% federal, payroll tax per state, Pay As You Go (PAYG) | Marketplace collection + remittance |
| Trade licensing | State-by-state — NSW Fair Trading, VIC Building Authority (VBA), QLD Building and Construction Commission (QBCC), WA Building and Energy, SA Consumer and Business Services | Pro vetting must validate per-state license |
| Building codes | National Construction Code (NCC) via Australian Building Codes Board (ABCB), state amendments | Codes engine |

## Responsibilities

(Generic responsibilities per template, plus AU-specific:)

- **Master-franchisee partner identification + steward**: identify, vet, negotiate, and steward Australian master-franchisee relationship per the locked international rollout model. Specialist franchise counsel engaged from Day 1.
- **State-by-state license matrix** integration into pro vetting (more complex than Canada province model — each state distinct).
- **National Construction Code (NCC)** ingestion via Australian Building Codes Board (ABCB) into Sherpa codes engine.
- **HIA + Master Builders Australia partnerships**: secure trade-association partnerships in Year 1.
