# FW Webb Target Contact List

## Purpose

Identify the **top 20 FW Webb branches in New England (NH, MA, ME, RI)** ranked by Sherpa Hub co-location fit, plus FW Webb HQ leadership for parent-company alignment. This is the working contact map for the Phyrom-led partnership pursuit (see `10-fw-webb-engagement-runbook.md`).

## Ranking criteria

Branches scored 1-5 on each dimension (max 25):

1. **Trade-pro density in market** — local concentration of plumbing, HVAC, electrical, fire-protection contractors
2. **Branch footprint** — physical sq-ft headroom for an ~800 sq-ft Sherpa Hub buildout
3. **Branch volume / pro-counter throughput** — proxies pro foot traffic FW Webb already delivers
4. **Geographic proximity to Hub #1 Atkinson NH (anchor pilot)** — operational adjacency for the pilot region
5. **Branch manager openness signal** — known relationships, NE Pro Council activity, FW Webb employee referrals (placeholder until intel collected)
