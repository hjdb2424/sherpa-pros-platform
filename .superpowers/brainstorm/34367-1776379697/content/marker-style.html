Progressive Detail (Zoom-Adaptive)

Markers change based on zoom level. Zoomed out = cluster count dots. Medium zoom = avatar circles. Zoomed in or tapped = expanded mini-card with name, rating, trade, and action button. Most polished, scales best with many pins.

💡 My Recommendation: Progressive Detail

New England has 10 hubs with potentially hundreds of pros. Cluster dots at zoom-out prevent clutter, avatar circles at medium zoom give recognition, and the expanded mini-card on tap gives enough info to act without opening a full page. This is how Uber shows driver info — progressive reveal as you engage.
