CATEGORY 1 — HJD Builders network (10+ slots — TEMPLATE for Phyrom to fill)

Why this category is highest warmth: these are people Phyrom already knows personally. No cold-outreach cost. Fastest yes/no. The angel check from a former HJD client is also an implicit endorsement of the founder narrative — every check from this list becomes a recruiting story.

Accreditation reminder (SEC Rule 501): an individual is an accredited investor if (a) net worth >$1M excluding primary residence, OR (b) income >$200K (or $300K joint with spouse) for the past 2 years and reasonable expectation of same in current year. Many HJD clients with second-home builds, full-custom dwellings, or recent business sales clear this bar — but Phyrom must NOT solicit or accept investment from anyone he doesn't have a reasonable basis to believe is accredited. Wefunder Reg CF round (per spec §6.4) is the path for non-accredited HJD clients who want to participate.
