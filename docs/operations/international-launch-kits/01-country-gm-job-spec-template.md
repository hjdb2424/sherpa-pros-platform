## Title

**Country General Manager — Sherpa Pros {{country}}**

## Reports to

- **Phase 4A (Years 1-2 of country launch):** Phyrom (Founder + Chief Executive Officer), direct.
- **Phase 4B+ (Year 3+ of country, after Sherpa Pros HQ Chief Operating Officer (COO) hire):** COO, with quarterly direct read-out to the CEO and Board.

## Location

- **In-country, on-site at `{{primary_metro}}`** for first 24 months of country launch (no remote, no out-of-country).
- Travel ~25-40% across `{{secondary_metros}}` and quarterly to United States headquarters (HQ).

## Employment type

- Full-time salaried employee of the local Sherpa Pros `{{country}}` operating entity.
- Equity participation via Sherpa Pros HQ (United States) parent stock plan.

## Compensation envelope

| Component | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Cash base | `{{currency}}` `{{base_low}}` – `{{base_high}}` | USD-equivalent ~$150-250K target |
| Annual performance bonus | 30% – 50% of base | Tied to country Gross Merchandise Value (GMV), pro count, and Net Promoter Score (NPS) targets |
| Equity grant | 0.25% – 0.50% of fully diluted Sherpa Pros HQ shares | 4-year vest, 1-year cliff |
| Benefits | Country-standard | Health, dental, retirement contribution per local norm |
| Relocation | Up to `{{currency}}` 75,000 if needed | Reasonable moving + temporary housing + spouse/family support |

## Required experience

- **10+ years** in trade-supply, construction marketplace, or construction-technology leadership in `{{country}}`.
- Demonstrated **Profit & Loss (P&L) ownership** of a regional or national line of business of `{{currency}}` 25M+ annual revenue.
- **Local trade industry network** — relationships with `{{trade_supply_majors}}`, `{{marketplace_majors}}`, and `{{trade_assoc_majors}}` (or equivalents).
- Ability to navigate `{{regulatory_regime}}` from Day 1 without ramp time.
- **English fluency required** plus `{{language_secondary}}` as applicable to launch metro.
- Bachelor's degree minimum; Master of Business Administration (MBA) or trade-industry executive program a plus.

## Preferred backgrounds

(Country-specific instantiations enumerate concrete prior employers — see country-specific specs.)

- Trade-supply pro-division leadership (regional or national chains).
- Construction-marketplace operating leadership (founders, country GMs, Vice Presidents (VPs) of operations).
- National trade-association senior staff (Executive Director or equivalent).
- Big-construction-tech country leadership (Procore, Buildertrend, ServiceTitan equivalents).

## Responsibilities

### Country P&L
- Own end-to-end Sherpa Pros `{{country}}` launch P&L: revenue, cost of revenue, operating expense, contribution margin, capital efficiency.
- Hit Year 1 launch targets for pro count, job count, GMV, take rate, and NPS as set by HQ.
- Quarterly Board read-out on country traction and risks.

### Country org build
- Hire and lead the **4-person country team** (per international plan): Country GM (self), Pro Operations Lead, Client Operations Lead, Marketing + Community Lead.
- Establish in-country office (or hub-and-spoke distributed model where appropriate).
- Build country-specific Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) anchored in HQ playbooks but adapted for local nuance.

### Regulatory + tax + legal compliance
- Operate country entity in compliance with `{{regulatory_regime}}` and all federal / provincial / state / regional rules.
- Manage relationships with country counsel, tax accountants, insurance brokers, and licensing bodies.
- Ensure data residency, payment compliance, worker classification, and trade licensing all are clean from Day 1.

### Country-specific marketing strategy
- Adapt national-to-international Sherpa Pros brand for `{{country}}` voice without fragmenting the umbrella brand.
- Lead in-country Public Relations (PR), partnerships, and founding-pro / founding-client recruiting.
- Own country social presence, founder-voice content syndication (Phyrom + Country GM), and trade-association sponsorship calendar.

### Master-franchisee development (where applicable)
- For markets where the international playbook calls for a master-franchisee partner (e.g., Australia, EU per the locked rollout plan), identify, vet, negotiate, and steward the master-franchisee relationship.
- Maintain Sherpa Pros HQ standards across all sub-franchised territory.

### Executive-team representation
- Sit on the Sherpa Pros HQ Country Council (monthly cross-country sync) once stood up Phase 4B.
- Represent country voice in HQ product roadmap and brand decisions.
- Share country-level learning patterns into the global playbook.

## Interview process (4 rounds)

| Round | Participants | Focus | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | CEO Phyrom (1:1 video) | Mission fit, P&L track record, integrity | 60 min |
| 2 | COO + (where present) Chief Financial Officer (CFO) | Operational leadership, country org build plan, financial chops | 90 min |
| 3 | Board panel (3 board members + 1 country advisor) | Strategic judgment, governance, risk | 90 min |
| 4 | Reference + final | 6 backchannel references (4 user-supplied, 2 backchannel via Phyrom network) + final offer call | 2 weeks |

## Decision authority + escalation

- **Single-signature spend authority** up to `{{currency}}` 50,000 per transaction.
- **Two-signature** with CEO (Phase 4A) or COO (Phase 4B+) for `{{currency}}` 50,001 – 250,000.
- **Board approval** for >`{{currency}}` 250,000.
- All hiring of country team members reviewed with HQ People function.

## Success criteria — Year 1

- 10-15 founding pros onboarded in `{{primary_metro}}` (mirrors US Phase 1 cohort design).
- 50+ founding clients matched, completed jobs, and Net Promoter Score (NPS) ≥ 60.
- Country entity, payment rails, codes ingestion, and trademark filings all complete.
- Master-franchisee partner signed (where applicable).
- Country team hired and operating SOPs documented.

## Success criteria — Year 2

- 3-4 metros live (`{{primary_metro}}` plus 2-3 from `{{secondary_metros}}`).
- Country GMV ≥ `{{currency}}` 5M annualized.
- Country contribution margin ≥ break-even.
- Local seed-equivalent funding round closed (where applicable).
