Full-Screen Map + Bottom Sheet

The Uber pattern. Map fills the entire viewport. Info appears in a draggable bottom sheet that slides up. Most immersive, most mobile-native. This IS the Uber experience.

Split Panel — List + Map

Google Maps / Airbnb pattern. List on left, map on right (stacks on mobile — list on top, map below, or toggle between). Good for browsing, less immersive than full-screen.

Hybrid — Full-Screen Mobile, Split Desktop

Best of both. Mobile gets the Uber full-screen map + bottom sheet (thumb-friendly, immersive). Desktop gets the split panel (more screen real estate for list + map). MAP/LIST toggle on mobile to switch views.

💡 My Recommendation: Hybrid

Sherpa Pros is mobile-first (contractors on job sites, homeowners in crisis). The full-screen map + bottom sheet IS the Uber magic on phones. But on desktop, a split panel uses the space better for browsing pros/jobs. The hybrid gives you the best UX on every screen size. Emergency dispatch always uses full-screen map regardless of device — urgency demands full immersion.
