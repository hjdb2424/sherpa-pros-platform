## Risk factors — transparent disclosure

**No franchise opportunity is risk-free. You should consider:**

- **Time to monthly cash-flow positive: 14–22 months.** You will not be net-positive in Year 1 in most scenarios.
- **Initial Term: 10 years.** This is a multi-year commitment — not a side investment.
- **Operator-not-investor:** Sherpa Pros does not permit absentee ownership in Phase 4 Years 1–3.
- **Sherpa Pros LLC is an emerging franchise system** — Year 1 of Franchise Disclosure Document. Early-stage franchisors carry execution risk that mature franchisors do not.
- **Item 19 substantiation rests on Hub #1 + industry-comparable benchmarks** — your individual results may differ.
- **Northeast labor market is tight.** Hub Manager + Hub Associate hiring is a meaningful operational risk.
- **Materials orchestration depends on supplier-network performance.** Single-supplier disruption can affect throughput.

**Read the full Item 17 risk disclosure in the FDD.**
