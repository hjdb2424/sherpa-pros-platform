Title

Canada Country General Manager (GM) — Sherpa Pros Canada

Reports to

  • Phase 4A (Year 1-2 Canada launch): Phyrom (Founder + Chief Executive Officer (CEO)), direct.
  • Phase 4B+: Sherpa Pros Chief Operating Officer (COO).

Location

  • Toronto, Ontario, on-site at the Sherpa Pros Canada office (location to be leased in downtown Toronto or Liberty Village).
  • Travel ~25-40% across Vancouver (British Columbia (BC)), Montreal (Quebec (QC)), and Calgary (Alberta (AB)); quarterly to Sherpa Pros United States headquarters (HQ).

Employment type

  • Full-time salaried employee of Sherpa Pros Canada Inc. (a Canadian numbered company under the Canada Business Corporations Act (CBCA) — see 10-canada-entity-formation-and-compliance.md).
  • Equity participation via Sherpa Pros HQ (United States parent) stock plan.

Required experience

  • 10+ years Canadian construction industry experience, OR 8+ years construction + 4+ years marketplace / Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) leadership.
  • Demonstrated Profit & Loss (P&L) ownership of a Canadian regional or national line of business of CAD $30M+ annual revenue.
  • Local trade industry network — direct relationships with: Home Depot Canada, RONA / Lowe's Canada, Patrick Morin (QC), Kent Building Supplies (Atlantic), Patrick Morin, Castle Building Centres, TIMBER MART, Home Hardware Stores, and the Canadian Home Builders' Association (CHBA) provincial federations.
  • Experience navigating Canada's regulatory regime (see below) without ramp time.
  • English fluency required. French (Quebec) working proficiency strongly preferred for Quebec market launch starting Year 2 Canada.
  • Bachelor's degree minimum; Canadian Master of Business Administration (MBA) (Rotman, Ivey, Schulich, Desautels, Sauder) a plus.
