Instantiated from 01-country-gm-job-spec-template.md. Australia (AU) is third international country, locked as Year 3. Master-franchisee model is the leading hypothesis for Australia given geography + market size.

Title

Australia Country General Manager (GM) — Sherpa Pros Australia

Reports to

  • Sherpa Pros Chief Operating Officer (COO).
  • Quarterly read-out to CEO Phyrom + Board.

Location

  • Sydney, New South Wales (NSW), on-site at Sherpa Pros Australia office (Surry Hills / Pyrmont preferred).
  • Travel ~30-50% across Melbourne (Victoria (VIC)), Brisbane (Queensland (QLD)), Perth (Western Australia (WA)), Adelaide (South Australia (SA)); semi-annual to United States headquarters (HQ).

Employment type

  • Full-time salaried employee of Sherpa Pros Australia Pty Ltd (a proprietary limited company under the Corporations Act 2001).
  • Equity participation via Sherpa Pros HQ stock plan.

Component Range Notes

Relocation Up to AUD $90,000 If relocating to Sydney metro

Required experience

  • 10+ years Australian construction industry leadership, OR 8+ years construction + 4+ years marketplace / Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) leadership.
  • Demonstrated Profit & Loss (P&L) ownership of AUD $30M+ annual revenue line of business.
  • Local trade industry network — relationships with Bunnings, Reece, Tradelink, Mitre 10, Total Tools, hipages, ServiceSeeking, Master Builders Australia, Housing Industry Association (HIA).
  • Day-1 ability to navigate Australian regulatory regime: Privacy Act 1988, Australian Consumer Law, Franchising Code of Conduct (mandatory).
  • Master-franchise governance experience strongly preferred (since Australia is the leading candidate for master-franchisee partnership).
  • English fluency required.
  • Bachelor's degree minimum; Australian Master of Business Administration (MBA) (Australian Graduate School of Management (AGSM), Melbourne Business School (MBS), Macquarie) a plus.
