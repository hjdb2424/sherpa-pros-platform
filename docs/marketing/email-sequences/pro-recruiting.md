# Pro Recruiting Email Sequence

**Audience:** Cold contractors who saw a flyer (Lowe's Pro, FW Webb, Rockler) or a LinkedIn post but did not respond.
**Goal:** Get them to apply for the Founding Pro beta (10 spots).
**Cadence:** D0, D2, D5, D9, D14 (5 emails over 14 days).
**From:** Phyrom (founder), phyrom@thesherpapros.com
**Sender voice:** First person, plainspoken, NH GC. No marketing-speak. 8th-grade reading level.
**Brand bible compliance:** Says — Licensed, Verified, Code-aware, Built by a contractor, Local, Jobs not leads. Never says — Wiseman, gig, task, Uber for X, AI-powered as headline, disrupt, revolutionize, jargon abbreviations.

## Notes for the operator

- Personalize `{{first_name}}` always. If first name is unknown, use "Hey there" — never "Dear Pro" or anything robotic.
- Send each email at 7:15am local time (before the workday starts). Tradespeople check email in the truck.
- If a recipient replies at any point, drop them out of the sequence immediately and route to Phyrom's inbox.
- If a recipient clicks the apply link but does not finish, send Email 4 with a different subject: "You started applying — finish in 3 minutes" instead of advancing the cadence.
- Track open + click rate on each email. Email 2 (the math) should be the highest-performing — if it is not, the math callout is buried.
