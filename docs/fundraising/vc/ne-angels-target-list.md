## Decision framework — when to take an angel check (Phyrom playbook)

| Signal | Read | Action |
|--------|------|--------|
| Angel writes within 2 weeks of first meeting | Strong commitment | Accept; ask for 2 referrals to other angels in their network |
| Angel suggests other angels in their network unprompted | Multiplier value | Accept even if check is small ($25K) — the network is the prize |
| Angel commits to specific check size + signs SAFE | Cleanest path | Accept |
| Angel offers advisory shares in lieu of check | We need cash, not equity grants in Phase 0 | Reject for Phase 0; revisit at Phase 1 |
| Angel wants special board observer rights for <$100K | Anti-dilution complexity for too little capital | Reject; offer quarterly investor update access only |
| Angel pushes for over-aggressive valuation cap (sub-$4M) | Trying to get cheap-deal Wefunder cap before our priced round | Reject; cap is set per FAQ Q23 in `docs/fundraising/wefunder/page-content.md` |
| Angel ghosts after first meeting + no follow-up within 30 days | Dead lead | Mark `PASS` in pipeline; don't chase |
| Angel asks for full Stripe Connect transaction-level access | Diligence is reasonable but excessive at this stage | Provide aggregate dashboard only; full data at term-sheet stage |

**Accreditation reminder (SEC Rule 501):** an individual is an accredited investor if (a) net worth >$1M excluding primary residence, OR (b) income >$200K (or $300K joint with spouse) for the past 2 years and reasonable expectation of same in current year. Many HJD clients with second-home builds, full-custom dwellings, or recent business sales clear this bar — but Phyrom must NOT solicit or accept investment from anyone he doesn't have a reasonable basis to believe is accredited. # Operating cadence

- **Weekly:** Phyrom + SDR review pipeline. Advance one row per category per week minimum.
- **Per-row commit:** Before any cold contact, run a 5-minute LinkedIn check for the angel's activity in last 90 days. Cite a specific recent post or investment in the opener — angels who get 100 cold pitches/month answer the one that proves the founder did 5 minutes of homework.
- **Wefunder coordination:** every Cat. 1 + Cat. 2 angel conversation closes with: *"Would you also consider co-investing $X via our Wefunder community round? It lets your wife / business partner / friends-and-family participate alongside you without you fronting the full amount."*
- **Founding Pro coordination (per spec §5.2):** every Cat. 1 sub or sub-of-sub angel ALSO gets the Founding Pro offer (5% take rate forever) — angel check + supply-side recruit in one conversation.
