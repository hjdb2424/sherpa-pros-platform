# Canada Country General Manager — Sherpa Pros Canada

> Instantiated from `01-country-gm-job-spec-template.md`. Canada is the **first international country**, locked as Year 1 of Phase 4 international expansion.

## Title

**Canada Country General Manager (GM) — Sherpa Pros Canada**

## Reports to

- **Phase 4A (Year 1-2 Canada launch):** Phyrom (Founder + Chief Executive Officer (CEO)), direct.
- **Phase 4B+:** Sherpa Pros Chief Operating Officer (COO).

## Location

- **Toronto, Ontario, on-site at the Sherpa Pros Canada office** (location to be leased in downtown Toronto or Liberty Village).
- Travel ~25-40% across Vancouver (British Columbia (BC)), Montreal (Quebec (QC)), and Calgary (Alberta (AB)); quarterly to Sherpa Pros United States headquarters (HQ).

## Employment type

- Full-time salaried employee of **Sherpa Pros Canada Inc.** (a Canadian numbered company under the Canada Business Corporations Act (CBCA) — see `10-canada-entity-formation-and-compliance.md`).
- Equity participation via Sherpa Pros HQ (United States parent) stock plan.

## Compensation envelope (Canada-specific)

| Component | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Cash base | **CAD $200,000 – $330,000** | USD-equivalent ≈ $150-250K at typical CAD/USD |
| Annual performance bonus | 30% – 50% of base | Tied to Canada GMV, pro count, NPS targets |
| Equity grant | 0.25% – 0.50% of fully diluted Sherpa Pros HQ shares | 4-year vest, 1-year cliff |

## Required experience

- **10+ years Canadian construction industry experience**, OR **8+ years construction + 4+ years marketplace / Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) leadership**.
- Demonstrated **Profit & Loss (P&L) ownership** of a Canadian regional or national line of business of CAD $30M+ annual revenue.
- **Local trade industry network** — direct relationships with: Home Depot Canada, RONA / Lowe's Canada, Patrick Morin (QC), Kent Building Supplies (Atlantic), Patrick Morin, Castle Building Centres, TIMBER MART, Home Hardware Stores, and the Canadian Home Builders' Association (CHBA) provincial federations.
- Experience navigating **Canada's regulatory regime** (see below) without ramp time.
- **English fluency required**. **French (Quebec) working proficiency strongly preferred** for Quebec market launch starting Year 2 Canada.
- Bachelor's degree minimum; Canadian Master of Business Administration (MBA) (Rotman, Ivey, Schulich, Desautels, Sauder) a plus.

## Canada regulatory regime — required day-1 knowledge

| Domain | Statute / regulator | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Data privacy | **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)** federal + provincial overlays (Quebec Law 25, BC Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), Alberta PIPA) | Pro and client personal data |
| Franchise regulation | **Provincial Franchise Acts** in Ontario (Arthur Wishart Act), Alberta (Franchises Act), Prince Edward Island (PEI), Manitoba, New Brunswick (NB) | Master-franchisee model triggers disclosure obligations |
| Worker classification | Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) common-law tests + Canadian Workers' Compensation Board / Workplace Safety and Insurance Board (WSIB) per province | Independent contractor (1099-equivalent) vs employee, mirrors US Phase 1 model |
| Workplace safety insurance | **Workplace Safety and Insurance Board (WSIB)** Ontario; equivalents in BC (WorkSafeBC), Alberta (WCB Alberta), QC (Commission des normes, de l'équité, de la santé et de la sécurité du travail (CNESST)) | Mandatory premium per province for any employee work |
| Tax | Goods and Services Tax (GST) federal 5% + provincial overlays — Harmonized Sales Tax (HST) 13% Ontario, Provincial Sales Tax (PST) BC, Quebec Sales Tax (QST) | Marketplace must collect + remit |
| Trade certification | Skilled Trades Ontario, Industry Training Authority (ITA) BC, Apprenticeship and Industry Training Alberta, Commission de la construction du Québec (CCQ) | Pro vetting must validate trade tickets |
