Hub #1 Atkinson NH - Hub Manager Job Specification

1. Position Overview

Title: Hub Manager - Sherpa Hub Atkinson Reports to: Phyrom (Founder), direct in Phase 1; transitions to Operations Director by Phase 3 Location: Atkinson, New Hampshire (on-site, 5 days per week with optional Saturday morning member-hours coverage) Employment type: Full-time, salaried, exempt Start date: Phase 1 Month 5 (target)

Total target compensation Year 1: $75,000 - $100,000 cash + benefits.

3. Required Experience

  • 5+ years operational management experience in trade-supply, retail-pro-desk, construction operations, or trade-supply branch leadership
  • Demonstrated P&L ownership at branch / store / department level (revenue, opex, gross margin, inventory turn)
  • Hands-on familiarity with at least 2 trade categories (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, framing, finish carpentry)
  • Inventory management experience (cycle counts, reorder triggers, supplier reconciliation, shrink control)
  • Direct customer-service experience with professional contractors (not residential retail)
  • Familiarity with point-of-sale + inventory management systems
  • Valid driver's license
  • NH-based or willing to relocate

4. Preferred Background

The ideal candidate comes from one of these backgrounds:

  • FW Webb branch manager or assistant branch manager (NH/MA/ME) - exact operational profile, knows the supplier mechanics, knows the customer
  • Lowe's Pro Desk lead (NE region) - pro-customer focused, P&L-aware, retail systems
  • Home Depot Pro Desk lead (NE region) - same as Lowe's
  • Independent supply-house counter manager (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, masonry) - tight-margin operations
  • Ex-construction-trade-business owner stepping out of full-time GC / sub work and looking for stable salary + ops role
  • NHHBA member-company branch / division manager transitioning out of field

5. Responsibilities

5.1 Hub daily operations

  • Open the warehouse side at 5:00am for materials staging
  • Coordinate the 4 daily Uber Direct dispatch waves (6:30am, 8:00am, 10:00am, 12:00pm)
  • Oversee showroom open at 7:00am for member access
  • Manage Hub Associate(s) (1 PT in Phase 1, 2 PT by Phase 2)
  • Close the Hub at 6:00pm with end-of-day inventory check + cash drop (if cash transactions occurred)

5.2 Member onboarding + community

  • Personally onboard every new Hub member in the first 90 days
  • Build relationships with member pros - know names, jobs, recurring needs
  • Run member NPS surveys monthly + close the loop on detractor feedback
  • Drive member referral program - target 60+ active members by end of Phase 1 Month 6

5.3 Materials staging coordination

  • Receive supplier deliveries (FW Webb, Grainger, Zinc-coordinated) and reconcile against hub_materials_staging manifest
  • Oversee staging build (job-labeled racks) for next-morning dispatch waves
  • Triage discrepancies (short shipments, wrong items) with HQ Operations
  • Coordinate returns processing every Wednesday + Friday supplier pickup

5.4 P&L ownership at Hub level

  • Own the Hub P&L (revenue, opex, capex)
  • Submit weekly per-Hub dashboard to HQ Operations every Friday by 5pm (auto-pre-filled from hub_metrics_daily, manager adds qualitative narrative)
  • Lead first-Tuesday monthly P&L review with Phyrom
  • Submit capex requests up to $5K (Hub Manager approves), above $5K to Phyrom for approval
  • Hit Year 1 target of ~$10,600/month revenue + breakeven trajectory by Year 2

5.5 FW Webb partnership liaison

  • Once FW Webb LOI signs (Phase 1 Month 4 hard deadline per integration spec section 4): become the primary day-to-day FW Webb branch contact
  • Track Sherpa pro foot-traffic uplift to FW Webb branches (partnership performance metric)
  • Track FW Webb default-supplier order volume through Sherpa Materials
  • Participate in quarterly partnership performance reviews

5.6 Training event coordination

  • Run 12 training events per year (1 per month, 2 in peak months) with manufacturer sponsors (Festool, Hilti, OSHA, Schluter, Milwaukee, EPA RRP, etc.)
  • Coordinate manufacturer reps, attendee registration, on-site logistics
  • Hit 12+ attendees per event target

5.7 Security + compliance

  • Enforce member-badge + keypad access control
  • Review camera footage weekly for security incidents
  • Run quarterly security drills (theft response, fire response, medical response)
  • Maintain insurance compliance (broker contact, certificate updates)

7. Screening Criteria

Pre-screen (resume + LinkedIn)

  • 5+ years branch / department / store management experience
  • P&L responsibility evidence
  • Trade-supply or pro-retail background (not residential big-box retail)
  • NH-based or commuting feasible to Atkinson NH

Phone screen (30 min)

  • Career trajectory + reason for considering this role
  • P&L sample - walk through last role's revenue + opex + key levers
  • Inventory management + supplier reconciliation experience
  • Customer-service style with professional pros (not consumer retail)
  • Salary expectation + compensation philosophy (base vs. bonus tolerance)
  • Earliest available start date

On-site interview (3 hours, at HJD HQ)

  • Tour of Hub buildout site - candidate reads the space, asks questions
  • Operational scenario walkthrough: "It's 6am, your first dispatch wave was supposed to leave at 6:30am, your dock-staged inventory is short on a critical job's materials. What do you do?"
  • P&L scenario: "Show me how you'd cut $1,500/month opex without breaking member NPS."
  • Member-conflict scenario: "A member pro is upset their materials weren't ready. Walk me through your conversation."
  • Discussion of compensation, benefits, growth path
  • Phyrom personal-fit read

Reference check (3 references, post-finalist selection)

  • 2 supervisors, 1 peer / direct report
  • Verify P&L ownership claims
  • Verify scope of responsibility
  • Probe for shrink-control / inventory-discipline anecdotes
  • Probe for hard-conversation handling (firing, pricing dispute, supplier dispute)

Final

  • Phyrom approval
  • Background check + employment verification
  • Offer letter

  • Day 1: Badges, accounts, payroll, IT, walkthrough of HJD HQ + Hub buildout site
  • Day 2-3: Sherpa Pros platform deep-dive (marketplace, pro app, dispatch, materials engine)
  • Day 4-5: Ride-along with Phyrom on member calls + sub introductions

Week 2 - Hub operations training

  • Day 1: POS + inventory system training
  • Day 2: Materials staging workflow + Uber Direct dispatch workflow
  • Day 3: Returns processing + supplier reconciliation
  • Day 4: Security playbook + access control + cycle count
  • Day 5: Member onboarding flow practice

Week 3 - Soft-open dry run

  • Day 1-2: Test transactions, test dispatch, test member checkin
  • Day 3-4: Run soft-open with 5 founding pros (invite-only)
  • Day 5: Punch-list capture + week-1 retro with Phyrom

Week 4 - Live operations + first weekly dashboard

  • Day 1-5: Run Hub at controlled-access (Founding Pros + Gold tier only)
  • Friday: Submit first weekly report to HQ Operations
