# Letter of Intent — Sherpa Pros LLC × {{partner}}

**Pilot Hub Co-Location Program (12-Month Pilot)**

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") is entered into as of {{effective_date}} by and between:

- **Sherpa Pros LLC**, a {{state_of_formation}} limited-liability company with principal office at {{sherpa_address}} ("**Sherpa Pros**"), and
- **{{partner}}**, a {{partner_state}} corporation with principal office at {{partner_address}} ("**{{partner_short}}**")

(each a "**Party**" and collectively the "**Parties**").

---

## 1. Pilot Scope

### 1.1 Pilot Program

The Parties intend to establish a **12-month pilot** (the "**Pilot**") in which Sherpa Pros operates Sherpa Hub locations co-located inside {{branch_count}} (default **five (5)**) {{partner_short}} branches, selected by mutual written agreement.

### 1.2 Pilot Branches

Pilot branch locations (each a "**Pilot Branch**") shall be selected jointly within sixty (60) days of LOI execution and documented in **Branch Site Schedules**. Substitutions permitted by mutual written consent if a branch's facilities cannot accommodate the Sherpa Hub footprint described in §2.1.

### 1.3 Pilot Term

The Pilot shall commence on the **earlier of** (i) the soft-open date of Pilot Hub #1, or (ii) the date six (6) months after LOI execution (the "**Pilot Start Date**"), and shall continue for twelve (12) months ("**Pilot Term**"), subject to renewal or expansion per §9.

### 1.4 Signature Deadline

This LOI must be executed by both Parties on or before **{{signature_deadline}}** (default: Phase 1 Month 4 of Sherpa Pros' GTM plan).

---

## 2. Sherpa Hub Footprint

### 2.1 Footprint [LEGAL — sub-lease vs license-to-occupy]

Each Sherpa Hub shall occupy approximately **800-1,500 rentable square feet** within or directly adjacent to its Pilot Branch. Exact dimensions, layout, entry/exit configuration, and adjacency to the {{partner_short}} pro counter shall be documented in the Branch Site Schedule.

### 2.2 Hub Functions

Each Sherpa Hub shall include, at minimum:
- Pro lounge / coffee / printing / phone area
- Sherpa Materials staging area (Top-200 SKU buffer)
- Sherpa Pros member check-in counter
- Hub Manager workstation
- Member meeting / training space (sized per branch)

### 2.3 Buildout

Sherpa Pros is solely responsible for Hub buildout costs (estimated $80K per Hub). Sherpa Pros shall use {{partner_short}}'s preferred contractor list where commercially reasonable.

---

## 3. Economics [LEGAL]

### 3.1 Sub-Lease Fee

Sherpa Pros shall pay {{partner_short}} a sub-lease fee of **$1,500 per Hub per month** during the Pilot Term, payable monthly in advance, beginning on each Hub's individual Hub Open Date. 3% annual escalator beginning Year 2.

### 3.2 Common-Area / Utilities

Sub-lease fee in §3.1 is **inclusive** of Hub-allocable share of building common-area maintenance, utilities (electric, water, HVAC, sewer), parking, exterior lighting, security monitoring, snow removal, and landscaping. Sherpa Pros responsible for Hub-internal janitorial + Hub-specific telecom/internet beyond branch baseline.

### 3.3 Sherpa Materials Preferred-Vendor Commitment

During the Pilot Term, in geographic regions surrounding each Pilot Branch:
- {{partner_short}} shall be designated **primary supply partner** for the Sherpa Materials engine for SKUs that {{partner_short}} stocks
- Sherpa Pros shall route orders to {{partner_short}} for any qualifying SKU where {{partner_short}}'s landed cost is **within 7%** of next-best supplier
- Target Year-1 Sherpa Materials orchestration volume per Hub: **$20,000/month minimum by pilot Month 6**, ramping to **$40,000/month by pilot Month 12**

---

## 4. Services Provided by {{partner_short}}

At no additional cost beyond §3.1, in respect of each Pilot Branch:
- Floor space matching the Branch Site Schedule
- Standard branch security monitoring + alarm extension to Hub footprint
- Restroom + breakroom access for members + Hub staff
- Loading-dock access during branch hours for Sherpa Materials inbound/outbound
- Branch parking access
- Reasonable cooperation in joint marketing within the Pilot Branch (subject to mutual content review)

---

## 5. Services Provided by Sherpa Pros

Sherpa Pros is solely responsible for:
- Hiring, employing, managing Hub Manager + all Hub staff (Sherpa Pros employees)
- Hub equipment, fixtures, furniture
- Hub-staged materials inventory (buffer + pickup-ready)
- All member acquisition, onboarding, engagement
- All Hub-side marketing materials
- Maintaining all Sherpa Pros operational software (Marketplace, Materials engine, Hub dashboard)
- Commercial general liability insurance with {{partner_short}} named as additional insured ($2M per occurrence / $4M aggregate minimum) [LEGAL: confirm coverage]

---

## 6. Sherpa Materials Integration [LEGAL — data sharing]

### 6.1 Order Routing

Sherpa Materials engine routes eligible orders to corresponding {{partner_short}} Pilot Branch under preferred-vendor terms (§3.3).

### 6.2 Three Fulfillment Modes per Hub

1. **Buffer-stocked** (40% of vol) — Sherpa Pros maintains buffer of high-velocity SKUs in Hub
2. **Counter-pickup** (35%) — Pro picks up at {{partner_short}} counter, Sherpa Materials pre-stages
3. **Staged-and-Uber-Direct** (25%) — Sherpa Materials stages at Hub, Uber Direct delivers to job site

### 6.3 Volume Reporting

Each month {{partner_short}} provides Sherpa Pros with anonymized Pilot-Branch-level summary data on Sherpa-Materials-attributed sales volume. Each month Sherpa Pros provides {{partner_short}} with Hub member count + materials throughput for the corresponding Branch Site.

---

## 7. Exclusivity (During Pilot Term) [LEGAL]

### 7.1 {{partner_short}} Exclusivity

During the Pilot Term, {{partner_short}} shall not host, sublease to, or enter into any analogous co-location partnership with any **competing pro-marketplace platform** (defined as any service that dispatches paid trade jobs to independent trade pros) in any Pilot Branch or within **25 miles** of any Pilot Branch.

### 7.2 Sherpa Pros Exclusivity

During the Pilot Term, Sherpa Pros shall not co-locate any Sherpa Hub with any **competing {{partner_category}} multi-branch wholesale distributor** ($100M+ annual revenue) within **25 miles** of any Pilot Branch.

### 7.3 Carve-outs

{{exclusive_terms}}. Default carve-outs: single-trade specialty distributors (e.g. fire-protection-only, controls-only) and DIY retail (Home Depot, Lowe's) are not "competing." [LEGAL: refine carve-out list per partner category]

---

## 8. Performance Gates

Parties shall jointly review Pilot performance against gates defined in `evaluation-metric-memo.md`, summarized:

- **Per-Hub**: 40+ active members by M6, 80+ by M12; member NPS >50; materials volume $20K+/mo by M6, $40K+/mo by M12; on-time delivery >95%
- **Network-wide**: {{branch_count}} Hubs operational by M6; cumulative members 200+ by M12; cumulative materials volume $1.5M+ by M12
- **Partner side**: Pilot-Branch foot-traffic increase >15% vs control branches; supply purchase increase >25% in pilot regions; branch NPS improvement >10 pts

Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs) held by joint executive sponsors.

---

## 9. Expansion Option

### 9.1 Post-Pilot Expansion

If Pilot meets or exceeds §8 gates, Parties intend to expand to **25 co-located Hubs** by end of Pilot-Year-2 and **50+ Hubs nationally** by Pilot-Year-4 (subject to definitive agreements).

### 9.2 Right of First Refusal

For each potential expansion Hub branch identified by Sherpa Pros, {{partner_short}} has 30-day ROFR on terms substantially similar to the Pilot. If {{partner_short}} declines or fails to respond, Sherpa Pros may pursue a standalone or alternate-host Hub.

### 9.3 If Pilot Underperforms

If Pilot meets less than 70% of §8 gates, Parties shall meet within 60 days of Pilot Term end to determine whether to (i) extend Pilot 6 months at revised gates, (ii) wind down per §10, or (iii) renegotiate.

---

## 10. Term, Termination, Renewal [LEGAL]

### 10.1 LOI Term

This LOI remains in effect from Effective Date until earliest of: (i) execution of definitive Master Services + Sub-Lease Agreement superseding this LOI; (ii) end of Pilot Term; (iii) termination per §10.2 / §10.3.

### 10.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate on 90 days' written notice if other Party materially breaches and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice.

### 10.3 Termination for Convenience

After Pilot Month 9, either Party may terminate on 180 days' written notice for any reason.

### 10.4 Hub Wind-Down

On termination, Sherpa Pros shall remove all Hub equipment, signage, and inventory and restore the Pilot Branch footprint within 90 days, normal wear-and-tear excepted.
