# The Wedge

---

# The problem: lead-gen wastes contractors' money.

Most homeowners' first stop is a platform built on a broken model — contractors pay to **bid** on leads, homeowners get matched to whoever paid the most, and licensed work ends up with people who shouldn't be doing it.

| Platform | What it costs the pro | Why it fails the homeowner |
|---|---|---|
| **Angi** | Lead fees of $30–$100 each, often shared with up to 40 competitors. Effective **$400–$800 per closed job**. | BBB un-accredited; 1,800+ complaints 2023–2026; 2.1/5 on Trustpilot; Vermont Attorney General settlement Oct 2025. |
| **Thumbtack** | Variable per-quote. Pros buy contact for jobs that often ghost. | Documented contractor-deposit-theft losses **$300–$1,600 per incident**; BBB pattern-of-complaints review Nov 2025. |
| **TaskRabbit** | **22.5% effective fee load** (15% service + 7.5% trust). | Doesn't cover licensed trades at all — no electrical, plumbing, HVAC, roofing. |
| **Handy** | Subscription + per-task. | Narrow trade coverage. No code intelligence. Deprioritized post-Angi acquisition. |

<span class="caption">Sources: Angi Inc. IR · BBB filings · Sitejabber · Trustpilot · Hook Agency contractor surveys · Vermont AG. Full citations in data room.</span>

# Boston specialty: 5 lanes Angi structurally can't serve.

Boston is a head-on lead-gen knife fight if we open everything. So we don't. We open **5 specialty lanes** the national platforms can't filter for.

| Lane | Why Angi can't filter for it | Tailwind |
|---|---|---|
| **Mass Save heat-pump installs** | Requires EPA cert + Mass Save Network listing | **$10,000+ rebate per home in 2026**; ~15K–25K installs/yr statewide |
| **EV charger installs** | Requires licensed electrician + often panel upgrade | National Grid Turnkey Program covers most install cost |
| **Electrical panel upgrades** | Requires permit + code-aware quote validation | Prerequisite for heat-pumps and EV chargers |
| **Old-house specialists** | Plaster · slate · masonry · lead-paint disclosure | Pre-1950 housing stock is most of inner Boston |
| **Triple-decker exteriors** | Boston-specific building type, lead-paint disclosure required | Tens of thousands of triple-deckers needing 20-yr cycle work |

Each lane = a **branded, defensible category.** Old-House Verified is filed for USPTO trademark in Phase 0.

| Tier | Fee | Path |
