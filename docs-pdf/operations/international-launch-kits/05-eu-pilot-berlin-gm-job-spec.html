EU pilot regulatory regime — required day-1 knowledge

Domain Statute / regulator Why it matters
Data privacy General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) EU-wide, Bundesdatenschutzgesetz (BDSG — German Federal Data Protection Act), state Data Protection Authority (Landesdatenschutzbeauftragte) per Bundesland Pro and client personal data; strict consent + data residency
Trade regulation Gewerbeordnung (GewO — German Trade Regulation Act) Operating any trade in Germany
Skilled-trade qualification Handwerksordnung (HwO — Tradesmen's Code) — Meisterbrief (master craftsman certificate) required for many regulated trades (Anlage A trades) Pro vetting must validate Meister credentials for ~40 regulated trades; Anlage B trades require less
Consumer protection EU Consumer Rights Directive transposed into Bürgerliches Gesetzbuch (BGB — German Civil Code), EU Digital Services Act (DSA), EU Digital Markets Act (DMA) where in scope Online marketplace consumer protection
Franchise regulation No statutory franchise law; voluntary Deutscher Franchiseverband (DFV) Code of Ethics Master-franchisee model governed by general contract law + DFV self-regulation
Worker classification Scheinselbständigkeit (false self-employment) tests, Sozialgesetzbuch IV (SGB IV — Social Code Book IV), Deutsche Rentenversicherung Bund Pro classification; high false-self-employment risk in Germany — specialist counsel mandatory
Tax Umsatzsteuer (VAT) 19% standard / 7% reduced, Körperschaftsteuer (Corporation Tax) 15% + Gewerbesteuer (trade tax) ~14-17%, EU One-Stop-Shop (OSS) for cross-border VAT Marketplace VAT collection + remittance; OSS simplifies EU rollout
Building codes DIN (Deutsches Institut für Normung — German Institute for Standardization) + per-Bundesland Bauordnung (BauO — state building code) + EU harmonized standards via CEN Codes engine
