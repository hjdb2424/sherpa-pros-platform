## 6. Risk Notes + Open Questions

- **Phyrom name validation is blocking.** Every `{{name}}` placeholder must be replaced with a real verified name before outreach. Phyrom owns this validation pass.
- **FW Webb LOI dependency.** P1-2 (Portland ME) candidate path requires FW Webb LOI signed by Hub Integration spec §4 deadline (Phase 1 Month 4). If LOI delayed, P1-2 outreach defers and pipeline tightens.
- **Massachusetts registration timing.** Several MA-based candidates (P2-7 Worcester, W-15 Plymouth) cannot receive FDD until MA confirmed as non-registration filing or appropriate exemption documented. Coordinate with attorney engagement (per `01-attorney-evaluation-matrix.md`) on MA filing strategy.
- **Capital validation gap.** Several candidates have estimated capital ranges that may or may not meet $200K liquid threshold. Stage 2 Discovery Call must surface this honestly; pipeline management tracks "capital-confirmed" subset separately from raw inquiry count.
- **Operator commitment pressure-test.** Several candidates (P2-6 post-business-sale, P2-7 association director) may have lifestyle-investor preferences vs operator commitment. Stage 2 script (per `05-7-stage-screening-templates.md`) explicitly addresses this; VPFD must enforce hard line.
- **Pipeline scarcity protection.** Target 12–15 candidates so 7-stage screening attrition (typical: 80% drop rate from inquiry to signed agreement per industry data) still yields 1+ signed Hub #2 franchisee by Month 18. If pipeline drops below 10 candidates, immediate sourcing intervention required (additional channels, paid franchise-broker engagement, IFA conference attendance).
