## 1. Channel Strategy — Per Platform

### 1.1 LinkedIn — Phyrom personal account (founder-led thought leadership)
- **Account:** Phyrom personal LinkedIn (NOT a Sherpa Pros brand page)
- **Cadence:** Mon / Wed / Fri 7:30am ET (per existing 39-post editorial)
- **Source of posts:** `docs/marketing/linkedin-editorial.md` — pull weekly from the 13-week table
- **Format mix:** ~50% text · 25% photo · 15% carousel PDF · 10% native video / Loom
- **No hashtags.** Founder voice in 2026 doesn't need them.
- **Links in first comment, never in body.**
- **Phyrom replies to every comment within 60 minutes of posting** (algorithm boost)
- **Goal:** founder follower growth (target 5,000 by Day 90), Wefunder soft-commit, Founding Pro inbound DMs
- **This 30-day calendar** assigns the existing editorial to Days 1–30 — see Section 3 below

### 1.2 Instagram — @sherpapros brand account (visual-first)
- **Account:** Sherpa Pros brand IG (not Phyrom personal)
- **Cadence:** 5–7 posts/wk + 5–7 stories/day. Mix: 3 reels, 2 carousel, 1–2 single photo per week
- **Best post times:** Tue/Wed/Thu 11am ET (homeowner browsing) + Sat 9am ET (weekend project planning)
- **Format mix:** Reels (60%) · Carousel (25%) · Single photo (10%) · Static + UGC repost (5%)
- **Stories:** behind-the-scenes daily — jobsite, fundraising progress, beta pro shoutouts. Use polls + question stickers.
- **Aspect ratio:** Reels 9:16 with safe-zone padding for the IG bottom bar; carousels 4:5 portrait (max real estate in feed)
- **Captions:** burned-in for reels (most users watch with sound off); written captions 100–150 words with light emojis only if Phyrom approves (default: no emojis per CLAUDE.md user pref)
- **Goal:** Founding Pro recruiting (warm DMs from contractor accounts), homeowner brand awareness, before/after social proof

### 1.3 TikTok — @sherpapros brand account (contractor-POV + Quick Job demos)
- **Account:** Sherpa Pros brand TikTok
- **Cadence:** 4–6 posts/wk. Higher tolerance for volume than IG (TikTok rewards quantity)
- **Best post times:** Tue/Wed/Thu 6–9pm ET (after-work scrolling)
- **Format:** 100% vertical 9:16 video, 15–90 sec, captions burned in, trending sound where on-brand
- **Hooks:** first 1.5 seconds decide everything. Lead with motion or a number on screen.
- **Voice:** Phyrom on camera (50% of posts) + beta Founding Pros (40%) + screen-recording demos (10%)
- **Goal:** viral-likely Quick Job demos ("watch a real toilet install in 2 min"), code-violation catch reels, Founding Pro spotlights

### 1.4 Facebook — same brand account, simulcast IG + longer text
- **Account:** Sherpa Pros brand Facebook page
- **Cadence:** 5–7 posts/wk (matches IG schedule via Metricool simulcast) + 1–2 longer-form text posts/wk targeting older homeowner demographic (50+)
- **Format mix:** Same reels + carousels + photos as IG, plus weekly long-form text posts (300–500 words) about Mass Save, EV charger rebates, code-aware quote walkthroughs
- **Best post times:** Tue/Wed/Thu 8am ET (older demo morning scroll) + Sun 7pm ET (weekend project planning)
- **Goal:** older homeowner reach (50+, primary home-improvement spender), Mass Save / National Grid rebate education, local NH/ME/MA community building

### 1.5 X (Twitter) — Phyrom personal + Sherpa Pros brand
- **Phyrom personal X:** real-time fundraising updates, industry takes, replies to construction-tech accounts
- **Sherpa Pros brand X:** product updates, Founding Pro shoutouts, customer wins (with permission)
- **Cadence:** Phyrom 1–3 posts/day (X rewards volume), brand 4–7 posts/wk
- **Format:** short text (under 280 chars in the hook tweet) · threads for deeper takes · 16:9 video clips for demos
- **Best post times:** 7am ET, 12pm ET, 6pm ET (3-touch daily rhythm)
- **Goal:** building-in-public fundraising signal, industry positioning vs Angi/Thumbtack, real-time engagement with VCs and other founders

### 1.6 YouTube — @sherpapros brand (long-form education + shorts)
- **Long-form (3–10 min):** 1 video/wk, Wed publish, 16:9
  - Topics: "How to read a code-aware quote," "Mass Save heat pump rebate walkthrough," "Behind the scenes of a Founding Pro week," "What 5% take rate means vs Angi $87 lead cost"
- **Shorts (15–60 sec):** 3–5/wk, repurpose TikTok / IG Reels (vertical 9:16)
- **Goal:** SEO-evergreen education content (long-form), top-of-funnel discovery (shorts)
