# Recruiting Strategy — 4 Country General Manager Searches

> Covers all four locked international country General Manager (GM) searches: Canada (Year 1 of Phase 4 international), United Kingdom (UK) (Year 2), Australia (AU) (Year 3), European Union (EU) pilot Berlin (Year 4-5).

## Goal

Hire one **Country General Manager (GM)** per country, in seat **6 months ahead of country launch**, with full local trade industry credibility and Day-1 regulatory fluency.

## Tiering of search firms

We evaluated three tiers of recruiting partners. Recommendation up front: **Tier 2 per country** for the right balance of economics, speed, and local depth.

### Tier 1 — Global elite (deepest networks, highest cost)

| Firm | Coverage | Strengths | Typical fee |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Heidrick & Struggles** | Global (60+ offices) | Brand-name access, deep board networks, strongest research bench | 33% of first-year cash compensation + retainer USD $80,000 – $120,000 per search |
| **Egon Zehnder** | Global (60+ offices) | Privately held, partner-led delivery, leadership-assessment depth | 33% of first-year cash compensation + retainer USD $80,000 – $120,000 per search |
| **Korn Ferry** | Global | Largest assessment library, integrated leadership consulting | 33% of first-year cash compensation + retainer USD $75,000 – $110,000 per search |

**Per-search all-in cost (Tier 1):** ~USD $150,000 – $250,000 (retainer + success fee + assessments + travel).

**When Tier 1 makes sense:** Sherpa Pros HQ COO search itself; Board-level recruits. Likely **overkill for country GM Phase 4A**.

### Tier 2 — Regional specialists (recommended)

| Country | Recommended firm | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Canada** | **Caldwell Partners** (Toronto HQ) | Largest Canada-headquartered executive search firm; deep construction + retail + B2B SaaS practices; Canadian board network |
| **United Kingdom** | **Russell Reynolds Associates UK** (London) — note: Tier 1 globally but UK practice priced as Tier 2 for non-FTSE roles | Deep UK construction + B2B SaaS bench; alt: **Odgers Berndtson UK** |
| **Australia** | **Watermark Search International** (Sydney + Melbourne) | Strong Australian executive search; deep construction + retail + tech bench; alt: **Heidrick Sydney** as Tier 1 fallback |
| **EU pilot (Berlin)** | **Heads! Group** (Munich + Berlin + DACH coverage) | Leading DACH executive search; deep construction-tech + Mittelstand bench; alt: **Egon Zehnder Frankfurt** |

**Per-search all-in cost (Tier 2):** ~USD $80,000 – $120,000 (lower retainers than Tier 1, faster turnaround, deeper local network for non-celebrity roles).

### Tier 3 — In-house direct sourcing

| Channel | When to use |
|---|---|
| Phyrom + COO direct sourcing on LinkedIn | Always run in parallel with whichever search firm |
| Warm intros via existing investors + advisors | High-leverage; lean heavily on Sherpa Pros cap-table and advisor network |
| Existing United States Sherpa Pros pro / partner network referrals | Lower hit rate but high cultural fit |
| Trade-association warm intros (e.g., CHBA introduces Canadian candidates) | Underrated channel — trade-body Executive Directors know everyone |

**Per-search all-in cost (Tier 3):** ~USD $20,000 – $40,000 (sourcer + assessment + travel; bulk of cost is opportunity cost of executive time).

## Recommendation

**Engage Tier 2 per country, run Tier 3 direct sourcing in parallel from Day 1.** Tier 1 only if Tier 2 search fails after 4 months.

## Per-country search budget

| Line item | Budget (USD) |
|---|---|
| Search firm retainer + success fee | $80,000 – $110,000 |
| Background checks + leadership assessments (Hogan, Predictive Index, etc.) | $5,000 – $8,000 |
| Travel for finalist meetings (executive + Phyrom + COO) | $8,000 – $15,000 |
| Reference + backchannel research | $2,000 – $5,000 |
| **Total per search** | **USD $95,000 – $140,000** |

**Per international country budget cap: USD $120,000.**

## Search timeline (per country)

| Week | Activity |
|---|---|
| Week 0 | Search firm engaged, search committee chartered (Phyrom + COO + 1 Board member + 1 country advisor) |
| Weeks 1-2 | Position spec finalized + signed off; long-list research |
| Weeks 3-6 | Long-list (40-60 candidates) → short-list (10-15) |
| Weeks 7-12 | Short-list interviews (Round 1 + 2); narrow to 4-5 finalists |
| Weeks 13-16 | Finalist interviews (Rounds 3 + 4) + reference + assessment |
| Weeks 17-20 | Offer + negotiation + signed Letter of Intent (LOI) |
| Weeks 21-24 | Notice period + start (or earlier if on-bench candidate) |

**Total: 4-6 months from engagement to in-seat.** Engage **6 months ahead of country launch** to hit 6-month-prior-to-launch in-seat target.

**Total 4-search investment across Phase 4 international: USD $380,000 – $560,000.** Within the $400-600K loaded country team budget envelope per the international plan.

## Search committee

- **CEO Phyrom** — chair (Phase 4A); voting member (Phase 4B+).
- **COO** — chair (Phase 4B+); voting member.
- **1 Board member** — rotating, ideally one with country experience (e.g., a Canada-experienced board member chairs Canada committee).
- **1 country advisor** — ex-trade-association leader or ex-trade-supply pro division leader from the country (paid USD $10,000 – $25,000 advisor honorarium per search).

## Risks + mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Right candidate has 6-12 month notice period (especially UK + EU) | Engage 9 months ahead, not 6, for UK + EU |
| Search firm fails after 4 months | Tier 1 fallback engaged; pre-screened in Tier 2 contract |
| Country pay band insufficient at top of market | Pre-cleared with Board; allow stretch to top of band + extra equity |
| GM accepts then ghosts pre-start | Standard 90-day clawback on signing bonus; aggressive pre-start onboarding |
| GM washes out in Year 1 | 90-day check-in with Board; clear performance gates at 90 / 180 / 365 days |
