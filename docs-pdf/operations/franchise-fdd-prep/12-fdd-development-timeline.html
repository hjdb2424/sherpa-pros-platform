FDD Development Timeline — Critical Path

0. Executive Summary

The Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD) is a 23-Item federal disclosure document. Drafting takes 9–12 months end-to-end including attorney engagement, audit firm engagement, content compilation, internal review, attorney review, audit firm review, and final assembly. This document specifies the 12-month critical path from attorney engagement (Month 1) to FDD Version 1.0 issuance (Month 11), followed by 90 days of priority state filings.

Locked deliverables:

  • FDD Version 1.0 issued: Month 11
  • Initial state filings begin: Month 12 (non-registration states first)
  • Phase 4 Year 1 franchise sales begin: Month 12+ in NH/ME/MA/VT
  • Phase 4 Year 2 priority registration filings: NY/RI/MD/VA
  • Phase 4 Year 3 remaining 10 registration states
