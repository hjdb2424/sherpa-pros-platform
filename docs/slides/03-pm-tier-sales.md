# Four pain points your team feels every single week.

## The property manager vendor problem

- **Vendor sourcing is chaos.** When a heating call comes in at 2 a.m., who gets it? Honest answer in most shops: a spreadsheet, a group text, and whoever picks up first. That is not a process. That is a coin flip.

- **Quality is inconsistent and invisible.** You have 40 vendors in rotation. Three of them are great. Five of them are slow. Some you suspect are overcharging. Nobody on your team can prove which is which because **nobody has the data in one place.**

- **Compliance is a January fire drill.** License, insurance, **W-9 (taxpayer ID form)**, **Certificate of Insurance (COI)** — chased by email every renewal. A lapsed COI on a vendor who damages a unit is a liability event waiting to happen.

- **Unit-level finance is invisible.** You know what you spent at the property level. You do not know what you spent **per unit**, what was **Operating Expense versus Capital Expenditure**, or what each work order did to **Net Operating Income (NOI)**. The data exists. It just lives in 40 different places.

**What your finance team gets:**
