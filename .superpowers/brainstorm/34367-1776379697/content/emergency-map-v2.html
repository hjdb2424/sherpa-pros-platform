B) Light Mode Map — Consistent Brand

Standard Google Map (light style) matching the rest of the app. White bottom sheet, blue route, clean shadows. Feels calmer, more professional. Consistent with the brand — same light feel as browsing pros. Route and pins still pop on the light map.

🤔 This one's a real trade-off

Dark = urgency, focus, Uber-at-2am energy. Emergency FEELS different from the rest of the app. Signals "this is serious."

Light = brand consistency, calmer UX, less anxiety for the homeowner. Some argue panic-inducing UI makes emergencies feel worse, not better. Uber actually uses light mode for most ride tracking.
