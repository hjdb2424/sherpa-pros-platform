# Hub #1 Atkinson NH - Hub Manager Recruiting Playbook

## Purpose

Operational playbook for sourcing, screening, and closing the Hub Manager #1 hire. Companion to `07-hub-manager-job-spec.md`.

### Phase 1 Month 2 - Source build
- [ ] Week 1: Build LinkedIn target list (50+ named candidates across categories A-F)

- [ ] Week 3: Send second wave of 25 outreach messages

### Phase 1 Month 3 - Phone screens
- [ ] Week 1: Phone screen all responding candidates (target: 8-12 phone screens completed)
- [ ] Week 2: Continue phone screens + advance top 3-5 to on-site
- [ ] Week 3: On-site interviews top 3 candidates
- [ ] Week 4: Reference checks on finalist

### Phase 1 Month 4 - Offer + close
- [ ] Week 1: Offer extended to finalist
- [ ] Week 2-4: Negotiate + close + signed offer
- [ ] If finalist declines: pivot to second-choice candidate immediately, offer Week 3

## 4. Phone Screen Question Bank (30 min)

### Opening (5 min)
1. Walk me through your current role - what does a typical day look like?
2. What drew you to consider this Sherpa Hub role?

### Operational depth (10 min)
3. Tell me about a time you made a P&L decision that had a measurable impact - what was the lever and what was the result?
4. How do you manage inventory shrink in a counter-and-warehouse environment?
5. Walk me through how you handle a short-shipment from a key supplier on a customer-promised delivery date.
6. How do you train a new associate on a counter / warehouse role - what's your day-1, day-5, day-30 framework?

### Customer + community (5 min)
7. Tell me about your strongest professional contractor customer relationship and what makes it work.
8. What would you do if a key member is upset about an inventory stockout that was your fault?

### Compensation + fit (5 min)
9. What's the salary range you're targeting?
10. What's your earliest available start date?

### Wrap (5 min)
11. What questions do you have for me?

## 5. On-Site Interview Question Bank (3 hr)

### Tour + observation (45 min)
- [ ] Walk through Hub buildout site - watch what candidate notices
- [ ] Ask: "If you were running this Hub day 1, what would you change about this layout?"

### Operational scenario interviews (90 min, 3 scenarios at 30 min each)

**Scenario 1: Materials dispatch failure**
"It's 5:45am. Your 6:30am Uber Direct wave is supposed to leave with materials for 4 jobs. Your dock-staged inventory shows you're short 1 critical item for the highest-revenue job of the day. Your supplier (FW Webb) doesn't open until 7:00am. Walk me through the next 90 minutes."

Looking for: triage, communication (pro on the affected job), backup-supplier thinking, escalation, recovery action, documentation for post-mortem.

**Scenario 2: Member-conflict resolution**
"A long-time Founding Pro just walked into the Hub at 11am, visibly upset. He paid for a kit that was supposed to be ready at 8am, and his crew has been sitting at the job site for 3 hours. Walk me through how you handle the next 5 minutes."

Looking for: emotional regulation, customer focus, accountability, recovery action (refund, credit, expedite), and follow-up plan.

**Scenario 3: P&L pressure**
"Three months in, your Hub is running 30% under revenue target and 15% over opex target. Phyrom asks you to identify $2,000/month in opex cuts without breaking member NPS. Walk me through your first 5 candidate cuts and how you'd evaluate each."

Looking for: structured thinking, business-case construction, member-impact awareness, willingness to make hard calls.

### P&L + strategy (30 min)
- [ ] Show candidate the integration spec section 9 Hub P&L model
- [ ] Ask: "Where do you see the biggest revenue lever and the biggest cost risk?"

### Founder + culture fit (15 min)
- [ ] Phyrom personal-fit conversation
- [ ] Long-term arc: "Where do you want to be in 3 years?" - listen for whether candidate sees themselves growing into Operations Director / multi-Hub role

## 6. Reference Check Template (3 references, 30 min each)

```
Hi [Reference], I'm checking references for [Candidate] who's interviewing for a Hub Manager role with us at Sherpa Pros. Do you have ~30 minutes to chat?

1. How long did you work with [Candidate] and what was your reporting relationship?
2. What were [Candidate]'s top 2 strengths?
3. What were 1-2 areas where [Candidate] had room to grow?
4. Walk me through a specific situation where [Candidate] demonstrated P&L ownership.
5. How did [Candidate] handle a difficult conversation - either with a customer, a supplier, or a direct report?
6. Did [Candidate] ever miss a critical commitment? How did they handle it?
7. Inventory accuracy + shrink - how did [Candidate] manage that?
8. Would you hire [Candidate] again? In what role?
9. Is there anything I should know that I haven't asked?

Thanks - this is very helpful. Confidential of course.
```

## 7. Decision Framework

**Green flags:**
- 5+ years branch / department management with demonstrable P&L
- Specific shrink-control + inventory-accuracy anecdotes
- Self-aware hard-conversation handling
- Energized by founding-team responsibility
- NH-rooted (commute is sustainable, community ties)

**Yellow flags:**
- Long tenure but no clear P&L ownership
- Excellent counter / customer skills but no team management
- Salary expectations 20%+ above range (probe for negotiability)

**Red flags (pass):**
- No P&L responsibility
- Career trajectory shows pattern of 1-2 year jumps
- Reference checks reveal accountability avoidance
- Compensation expectations 30%+ above range with no flexibility
- Cannot articulate why this role vs. their current role

## 8. Backup Plan if Search Stalls

If by Phase 1 Month 4 no offer is signed:

### Option 1: Extend search 30 days
- [ ] Phyrom personally takes Hub Manager interim role for first 60 days of operations
- [ ] Continue search with broader geographic range (open to relocation candidates from MA, ME, VT)
- [ ] Increase salary range to $85-95K to widen pipeline

### Option 2: Hire interim Hub Manager (contractor)
- [ ] Engage a contractor / interim manager from Robert Half or a construction-ops-specific recruiting firm for 90 days
- [ ] Use the 90 days to continue full-time hire search at the right level
- [ ] Soft-open proceeds on schedule

### Option 3: Phyrom runs the Hub for 90 days
- [ ] Acceptable Phase 1 contingency given Phyrom is on-site at HJD HQ
- [ ] Adds load to Phyrom but keeps soft-open + grand-open on schedule
- [ ] Continue hire search in parallel
- [ ] Hub Manager hired by end of Phase 1 Month 6 / start of Phase 2
