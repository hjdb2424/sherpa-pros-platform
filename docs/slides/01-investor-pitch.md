# The problem: lead-gen wastes contractors' money.

Most homeowners' first stop is a platform built on a broken model — contractors pay to **bid** on leads, homeowners get matched to whoever paid the most, and licensed work ends up with people who shouldn't be doing it.

| Platform | What it costs the pro | Why it fails the homeowner |
|---|---|---|
| **Angi** | Lead fees of **$30–$100 each, often shared with up to 40 competitors**; effective **$400–$800 per closed job** | BBB un-accredited; 1,800+ complaints 2023–2026; 2.1/5 on Trustpilot; VT Attorney General settlement Oct 2025 |
| **Thumbtack** | Variable per-quote pricing; pros buy contact for jobs that often ghost | Documented contractor-deposit-theft losses **$300–$1,600 per incident**; BBB pattern-of-complaints review Nov 2025 |
| **TaskRabbit** | **22.5% effective fee load** (15% service + 7.5% trust) | Doesn't cover licensed trades at all — no electrical, plumbing, HVAC, roofing |
| **Handy** | Subscription + per-task | Narrow trade coverage; no code intelligence; deprioritized post-Angi acquisition |

<span class="small">Sources: Angi Inc. IR, BBB filings, Sitejabber, Trustpilot, Hook Agency contractor surveys, Vermont AG. Full citations in data room.</span>

# Business model: three revenue lines.

| Revenue line | Beta pricing | Standard pricing | Why it works |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Marketplace take rate** (the floor) | 5% of completed job | **10% of completed job** | Roughly half a contractor's all-in Angi cost. Tied to outcome, not bid-clicks. |
| **Pro subscription** (recurring) | $0 | **$49 / month per pro** | Predictable Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR). Funded by the value of vetted, code-checked jobs. |
| **Property Manager tier** (enterprise) | — | **$4 / unit / month entry, dropping to $1.50 / unit / month at scale (5,000+ units)** | Multi-year contracts. Net-Operating-Income (NOI) impacting product. PM industry currently pays ~$4/unit in spreadsheet-and-text-message overhead. |

<br>

**Founding Pros** — the first 10–12 beta pros — keep **5% take forever**. Permanent recruiting hook. Permanent loyalty lock against any future competitor.

<br>

**Unit economics, back-of-envelope:** 200 pros at standard pricing ($49 sub + 10% take on $8K Gross Merchandise Value per pro per month) = **$2.0M Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) before the PM tier turns on**. PM tier adds enterprise upside with multi-year contracts.
